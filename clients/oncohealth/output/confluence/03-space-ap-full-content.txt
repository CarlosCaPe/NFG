Databricks 

Databricks is a spark based processing tool that we use for most of our analytics. 

black1solidInstance of Databricks

We have 3 instances of databricks. One (oaspark) is our development engine. This is where we do our research and development along with any processes that are sporadic or inconsistent (Impact Analysis). The second is our testing environment as well as the storage location for some of our powerBI dashboards (oaspark2). The third (insights) is our production engine. It contains both the production versions of the CDR and Oncology Insights. There are much stricter regulations on this server and it's much less flexible. In each instance the folder structure for code is basically the same:

Delta Lake

Delta Lake is databrick's version of a data lake with the addition of versioning and the speed of spark. The data is structure in a specific format and then tables are built on top for use. This allows the underlying data to be separate from the usable data, which adds another layer of security. 

We have two lake structures, one for the  and one for  tables. More detailed information about these can be found on their respective pages. 

PowerBI

PowerBI is our visualization tool of choice. We've developed a huge suite of dashboards and reports for use by internal and external clients. Everyone who wants to access our dashboards needs an account. These are requested through Technology. To build reports, a pro account is needed.

#### Making a template
Labels: featured, documentation-space-sample

Give your authors a helping hand by using templates in your documentation space. You will need Space Administrator permissions to create templates.

To create a template:

Go to "Space Tools" in the sidebar, select "Content Tools" and create a new template.
Click "Page Layout" and add sections and columns to your page.
Add headings and sub-headings as needed.
Choose "Instructional Text" from the "Template" menu and add text that is only visible in the editor.
Save your template.
Your template will become available in the Create dialog for this space.
